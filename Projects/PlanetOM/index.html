Planet OM

Set off on a transformative journey through the soundscapes of Planet OM.

Planet OM is a electronic world music project produced and composed by Israeli artist Gidi Cohen. Planet Om is an independent production that combines a wide range of genres with elements from electronic music and Middle Eastern-psychedelic influences, in collaboration with other musicians on the Jerusalem-Tel Aviv axis.

About Me

Led by Israeli keyboardist and producer Gidi Cohen, The Planet Om project offers a cinematic journey through a world where electronic music intertwines with global influences. This fusion creates grand, harmonious landscapes intended to transport listeners to otherworldly realms. Each track is a vibrant exploration of sound, offering a unique sonic adventure. Om, the sound of creation, embraces a wide variety of styles, whether you’re looking to unwind, dance, or explore new musical horizons.
